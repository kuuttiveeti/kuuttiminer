FAQ

Who is behind this site?

I'm Veeti, 22-year-old student from Finland. I really love Saimaa Ringed Seals.

How does TheSealSaver work?

TheSealSaver uses your computing power to validate Monero cryptocurrency transactions. This so called mining is rewarded with Monero cryptocurrency which is eventually converted to Euros and donated to the WWF. TheSealSaver uses CoinHive to do the magic in your browser.

How much of my CPU the mining uses?

Mining uses your CPU only with 40% so you have plenty of resources left to watch cat videos and NorppaLive.

Where does the money go to?

Money generated through TheSealSaver will be donated 100% to WWF Saimaa Ringed Seal charity.

I personally coverage all the administrative and other fees from running this website.

What about fees that are in the process?

There are some small fees that occur while transfering Monero to Euros. I personally coverage these fees by adding in the same amount money that fees take.

How do I know that money goes to seals?

What else I can do?

You can donate straight to Ringed Seals through WWF! Check out more information at WWF website for Saimaa Ringed Seals. Saimaa Ringed Seal only faces existential threaths from humans, so you can also raise awareness for responsible fishing in Saimaa area.
